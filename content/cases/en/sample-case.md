A growing services firm was dealing with constant urgency. Each new request displaced the previous priority, creating drift instead of progress.

The intervention focused on three moves:

- resetting priority criteria,
- clarifying ownership by stage,
- establishing a weekly review cadence.

The result was a steadier operation, fewer reactive changes, and better conditions to introduce future improvements.
