Growth rarely breaks operations all at once. More often, friction shows up as repeated clarification, unstable priorities, and over-reliance on a few key people.

Three signals usually appear early:

- The same information is requested repeatedly.
- Priorities shift without a shared rule.
- The team advances mainly through urgency.

Before adding more tools, review the process design, the decision sequence, and who owns each step.
